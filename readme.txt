== Description ==
<h1>Change Out of Stock Text for Woocommerce</h1>

<p>This plugin lets you change the text in your Woocommerce store that describes the availability of your products.</p>
<br>
<p>This free version will let you alter the default text for when a product is in stock, out of stock or low stock. You can include the current stock of a product by typing $stock$ within the text, to make this work make sure your products have a stock quantity.</p>



<h2>Priorities</h2>

<p>Leaving a setting blank will mean other text is used, the priorities of options are below. If backorders are allowed, the in stock text will be used.</p>

<ul>
  <li>In stock: (your in stock default text)->(Woocommerce in stock default text)</li>
  <li>Out of stock: (your out of stock default text)->(woocommerce default out of stock default text)</li>
  <li>Low stock: (your low stock text)->(your in stock text)->(woocommerce low stock text)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Pro Version</h3>

<p>Once released the pro version will add more options, allowing you to better control availability information. These include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Giving specific text to products</li>
  <li>Adding links to your text</li>
  <li>Specifying the text displayed when backorders are allowed</li>
  <li>Specifying the text used to notify a user when a backorder is used</li>
</ul>
